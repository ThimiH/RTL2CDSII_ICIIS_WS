\chapter{Placement and Power Planning}
\label{ch:placement}

Following Week 11 tutorial, this chapter will detail placement strategies, power planning (power rails, power domains, decoupling), and preparing for routing.

\section{Installation}
Tools: OpenROAD/OpenLane (for open flow) or commercial place-and-route tools.

\section{Steps}
1. Prepare LEF/DEF and floorplan.
2. Define power rails and power domains.
3. Run placement and capture utilization/area reports.

\section{Deliverables}
- Floorplan images
- Placement reports and power netlist
